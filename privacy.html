Privacy Policy

Information We Process

This decentralized digital interface does not collect or store personal information. Public blockchain addresses may be processed as part of standard blockchain interactions. All blockchain data is publicly available by design.

Use of Information

Public blockchain addresses are used solely to facilitate decentralized network interactions. No personal identifiers are linked to these addresses by this interface.

Third-Party Services

This interface may interact with decentralized networks and protocols. These interactions are processed according to the respective network protocols and are not controlled by this interface. Users should review the terms and policies of any networks they choose to interact with.

User Rights

Users maintain full control over their blockchain addresses and any actions taken through this interface. Due to the decentralized nature of blockchain networks, certain data may be immutably recorded on public ledgers.
